About CAUVERY Bhavan

CAUVERY Bhavan at NIT Kurukshetra provides a safe, comfortable, and student-friendly living environment with essential facilities for academic and personal growth.

Facilities Provided

  • 24/7 Security and CCTV Surveillance
  • High-speed WiFi connectivity
  • Clean and hygienic mess facility
  • Regular housekeeping and maintenance
  • Electricity and water supply
  • Common study and recreation areas

Hostel Fee Details

  • Single Room: ₹50,000/year
  • Double Sharing: ₹35,000/year
  • Triple Sharing: ₹25,000/year

Do's

  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas
  • Follow hostel timings and rules strictly
  • Respect fellow residents and staff
  • Carry ID card at all times
  • Inform warden before leaving hostel

Don'ts

  • Do not damage hostel property
  • No loud noise or disturbance after quiet hours
  • Do not allow unauthorized visitors
  • No smoking or alcohol inside premises
  • Aoid wasting water and electricity
